The Day We Met

Not the day I was screaming behind you, but the day we met at Meltdown 2024. I didn't really know you, and you didn't really know me. It was our first time meeting in real life, outside of occassionally gayming together. I remember getting off the bus outside the British Library with you and walking home in different directions. Who knew that one day we'd be walking home together almost everyday?

Our First Date

The catastrophe of Blooms2026. We were shopping for Cacey's birthday gift and something inside of me felt different. I knew that I liked you. The next day you spent hours with me. Comforting me, eating with me, watching Wicked together and just yapping. The very next day? You FINALLY asked me out on a date. Bone Daddies, running away from Chloe, talking about Hamilton - the works. I was head over heels for you and you didn't know.

Made it Official

Picture perfect day. I was giddy from the start, spending hours getting ready and you picked me up with a gorgeous bougquet. We walked to Chayan, got some boba, walked to dinner and had yummy food, and finally Hamilton (or so I thought).. I had never been so grateful in my life, but I didn't know things would get better. We walked across from Big Ben and you pulled out another flower from your bag, how it didn't get crushed? I don't know. And you asked me to be yours. I wanted to cry, I wanted to jump around and tell the whole world. And of course, we went for food right after.

The Moment I Knew

It was like a feeling that was slowly crawling into my heart. But, on a random day, I woke up next to you, on your bed and thought that I couldn't imagine life without you, that I'd want to wake up next to you everyday. I just knew. And it slipped out of my mouth the very next day.
